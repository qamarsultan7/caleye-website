Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CalEye

How accurate is the AI food recognition?

CalEye's AI has been trained on over 50,000 food photos and achieves 99.2% accuracy on common foods. For the remaining edge cases, you can manually adjust portions and ingredients. We continuously improve accuracy with each scan.

Does CalEye work with all types of food?

CalEye recognizes 5000+ food types globally, including cuisines from around the world, home-cooked meals, restaurant dishes, packaged foods, and more. It excels with mixed dishes and complex meals that would be tedious to log manually.

Can I use CalEye without an internet connection?

Yes! CalEye's core features work completely offline. You can scan meals, view your history, and track progress without internet. Your data automatically syncs when you reconnect to the network.

How does CalEye handle my privacy?

Your food photos are never permanently stored on our servers. AI processing is ephemeral—the image is processed and then deleted. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. You own your data and can export it anytime.

When will CalEye be available on iOS?

CalEye is coming to iOS soon! Android users can download now from Google Play. Sign up on our website to get notified the moment the iOS app launches.

Is there a free version or do I need to pay?

CalEye is completely free to download and use. You get 10 AI scans per day on the free tier, plus full access to history, goals tracking, and offline features. A Pro tier (coming soon) will offer unlimited scans and additional coaching features.
