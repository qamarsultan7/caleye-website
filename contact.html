Need help with food scan results, app behavior, or policy questions? Our support team is here to assist.

We read and respond to all inquiries. Choose the best option for your question:

  • Business & Partnerships:

📋 What to Include

Help us assist you faster by including:

  • ✓ A clear description of your issue or question
  • ✓ Your platform (iOS, Android, or Web)
  • ✓ App version (found in Settings)
  • ✓ Steps to reproduce the issue
  • ✓ Screenshots if available
  • ✓ The food image (if it's a recognition issue)
