Privacy Policy

How CalEye protects your data and respects your privacy.

Last updated: March 23, 2026

📸 Your food photos are processed ephemerally and never stored on our servers. CalEye processes images in real-time for AI analysis and then deletes them. We never build a permanent food database from your meals.

1. Information We Collect

CalEye collects minimal information. Here's what we process:

Uploads & Scans

  • Food images you upload for AI analysis
  • Optional scan metadata (time, location hints if provided)
  • Your nutrition data responses (edits, macro goals)

Device & Usage

  • Basic technical telemetry (app version, OS version, error logs)
  • Feature usage analytics to improve product
  • Device identifiers for crash reporting

What we do NOT collect: We do not require account creation, email, or personal identity information to use CalEye. No advertising tracking. No location history.

2. How We Process Your Data

All data processing is designed with privacy-by-default principles:

Ephemeral Processing

  • Food images are processed immediately for AI analysis
  • Images are NOT permanently persisted to database
  • Processing logs are cleaned up within 24 hours

AI Analysis

  • Your image is sent to AI inference engines only for real-time food recognition
  • Results (calories, macros) are returned to your app
  • Original image is not retained by AI providers

Local Storage

  • Meal logs are stored locally on your device
  • Optional cloud sync uses end-to-end encryption
  • You control what syncs and what stays on-device

3. Data Storage & Retention

Photos

Food photos are NOT stored on CalEye servers. Processing is ephemeral. Each image is analyzed and then purged.

Nutrition Logs

If you enable cloud sync, your meal logs (dates, foods, nutrition data) are encrypted and stored. You can delete this data anytime from app settings.

Technical Logs

Error logs and usage analytics are kept for up to 30 days for diagnostic purposes, then deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

CalEye partners with trusted services to deliver nutrition analysis:

  • AI Image Recognition: For food identification (Google Cloud Vision, similar)
  • Nutrition Databases: For macro and micronutrient lookup (USDA FoodData Central)
  • Cloud Infrastructure: For app backend and optional data sync

These partners process data only as needed to return results. They operate under their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights & Control

Access

You can export all your data anytime from app Settings → Data Export.

Deletion

Delete individual meals, your entire log, or disable cloud sync. All deletion is immediate and permanent.

Opt-Out

You can disable analytics telemetry in app Settings without losing core functionality.

No Advertising

CalEye does not use your nutrition data for targeted ads or share it with advertisers.

6. Security

CalEye employs industry-standard security practices:

  • All data in transit uses TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Cloud data at rest uses AES-256 encryption
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • No unencrypted personal nutritio data stored

7. Children's Privacy

CalEye is not intended for users under 13. If we discover a child has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Significant changes will be notified through the app. Continued use of CalEye after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? We're here to help:

  • Email: privacy@caleye.app
  • Support: Contact page
  • Response time: 1-2 business days
